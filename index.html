About Us

Bauble is a free platform that acts as a bridge between colleges and high school students. We have students from colleges all over India working with us who can clear any doubts high school students have about the college they are looking to join. Right now we're confined to engineering- we'll be moving on to other domains very soon! We have helped over 150 students and their parents with queries within two days of launch. Almost all the people who have used our platform reported that they were extremely satisfied with the answers/information we had given them.



Examples of queries raised in our platform:


1. Should I take CSE(or any other course for that matter) in college A or college B?
2. Given my options(Colleges A, B, C), which would be the best choice for me?
3. Which course in my undergrad would give me a best chance if I want to pursue an MS/MBA in the future?
